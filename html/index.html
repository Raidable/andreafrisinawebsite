ANDREA FRISINA

I see and live my life as a collection of stories, constantly unfolding.

Every day is a story, and every moment can carry a different one.

Through my camera, I turn these fragments into films, shaping real stories with a beginning and an end, both in my personal work and in projects for clients.

What I create are memories, stories meant to be lived again.
